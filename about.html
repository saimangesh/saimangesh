Pre-crop dynamics

Pre-Processing Stage. In this stage farmers face a problem as to which crop to grow. So we are here to suggest the farmers with the crops that will give them the best yield based on their soil quality and water availability.

Ongoing-crop dynamics

In the processing stage farmers face a problem as to what are the best fertilizers and where to buy them so we are here with the solution of online marketing where will be giving them the best fertilizers are the least possible cost.
